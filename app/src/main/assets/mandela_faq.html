• WHAT IS MANDELA CLUB PROPERTIES?
    • Mandela Premium Club Properties ltd. Happiness Stock Exchange (MCLP) is a one of its kind program for Mandela Premium Club Properties Ltd. customers, where they are recognized and rewarded through ‘Happiness Shares’, right from the ‘Expression of Interest’, to handover, throughout the lifecycle of their project.
  • WHO CAN JOIN THE PROGRAM?
    • The program is currently open to only customers of select Mandela Premium Club Properties projects, who are legal residents of India who are at least 18 years old and the same is limited to one (1) account per individual.
    • Customers have to go through a verification process through the MCLP contact center before one can become a member of the program.
  • HOW DO I JOIN THE PROGRAM?
    • Once you provide the ‘Expression of Interest (EOI)’ or booking, you would receive your MCLP welcome kit from your relationship manager. The kit will guide you with the instructions to access the program through the app and the microsite with the detailed features of the program to refer and use.
    • Enrolment will be done by your GPL relationship manager.
    • You must agree to the program terms and conditions to get enrolled into the program.
  • WHAT AM I AGREEING TO, BY JOINING THE PROGRAM?
    • By joining the program, you agree that you have read, understood and agreed to be bound by the Terms and Conditions and by any changes or modifications we may make to the program terms and conditions. You will agree to receive SMS/email communications from us from time to time.
  • HOW WILL YOU USE THE INFORMATION I PROVIDE THROUGH THE PROGRAM?
    • The personal information collected from you in connection with the program is subject to our Privacy Policy. You will be informed of all schemes which you will be eligible for, based on your location.
  • HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK?
    • Once you become a member of MCLP, you can download the MCLP mobile app and login to the same (available in both Android and iOS) or website and start interacting with us.
    • You get to earn happiness shares from time to time, for undertaking various activities listed in the program’s “Earn Happiness Shares”, right from the time of EOI till handover.
    • Once you have saved up enough shares, the same can be redeemed for rewards from a catalogue of attractive gifts and digital redemption vouchers.
  • HOW DO I EARN HAPPINESS SHARES?
    • You earn happiness shares right form showing, EOI, completing booking and registration, and for undertaking a host of other activities till handover, listed under the program’s “Earn Happiness Shares”.
  • WHAT ARE THE REWARDS?
    • For all the activities you undertake throughout the project lifecycle, you would be rewarded with happiness shares, which you can redeem for rewards in the form of gifts and digital redemption vouchers from popular brands.
  • HOW DO I REDEEM HAPPINESS SHARES FOR REWARDS?
    • Once you have saved up enough happiness shares, the shares can be redeemed from a catalogue of attractive gifts or digital redemption vouchers listed under the “Use your shares” tab.
    • You can choose the products, commensurate to the happiness shares earned by you and order the same in the MCLP website or mobile app.
    • Rewards cannot be exchanged or returned for another product or service or for cash refund. No cash top-ups (part cash, part happiness shares payment) are allowed while redeeming rewards.
    • You need to reach a minimum of ¬4000 happiness shares before you can start redeeming the same.
    • Happiness shares used to redeem a reward will be deducted from the total happiness shares available in your member account.
  • HOW SOON WILL MY HAPPINESS SHARES BE CREDITED AFTER I COMPLETE BOOKING?
    • The happiness shares will show up within two working days of your booking.
  • WHAT IF MY HAPPINESS SHARES ARE NOT SHOWING IN MY ACCOUNT?
    • If you have concerns that a transaction or any other action is not being properly applied to your member account, you can raise a query to a GPL representative through the “Contact Us” section in the MCLP mobile app or website.
  • DOES MY MEMBERSHIP EVER EXPIRE?
    • Membership status will be deactivated on your request to your GPL relationship manager.
    • Mandela Premium Club Properties will have the right to activate or deactivate your membership based on certain parameters.
  • CAN MY MEMBERSHIP BE REVOKED?
    • Mandela Premium Club Properties Ltd. has the sole discretion to revoke your membership or even take appropriate legal action if we suspect fraud, misrepresentation, abuse, or violation of the program terms.
  • WHAT HAPPENS TO MY HAPPINESS SHARES IF MY MEMBERSHIP IS REVOKED?
    • If your membership is revoked, any happiness shares or rewards in your account or available to you prior to such termination will automatically expire, and your access to the program and its features will automatically terminate.
  • CAN THE PROGRAM BE MODIFIED OR TERMINATED?
    • Mandela Premium Club Properties Ltd. has the right to alter, change or modify the Terms and Conditions from time to time due to business or legal reasons. Mandela Premium Club Properties may also suspend or terminate this program at any time, for any reason, including any legal requirement. To the extent possible, Mandela Premium Club Properties shall endeavor to notify each member in advance of such a termination.
  • WHAT IF I FORGET MY PASSWORD?
    • If you have forgotten your password, click on the “Forgot Password” option and enter your registered mobile number.
    • You will receive an OTP on your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP.
    • You will get a New Password window. Enter your new password and then re-enter the new password to confirm.
    • Click on “Submit”. Your new password will be updated.
  • HOW DO I TERMINATE / CANCEL MY PROGRAM MEMBERSHIP?
    • If you decide you no longer want to be part of the program, you may cancel your membership by contacting your GPL relationship manager.
  • WHAT HAPPENS TO MY HAPPINESS SHARES IF I TERMINATE / CANCEL MY PROGRAM MEMBERSHIP?
    • If you cancel your membership, you forfeit any happiness shares you may have earned and/or any rewards that might otherwise have been available to you during your continued participation in the program.
    • If you wish to return to the program after cancelling your membership, you will be required to join the program again, starting with Zero happiness shares or any available reward.
